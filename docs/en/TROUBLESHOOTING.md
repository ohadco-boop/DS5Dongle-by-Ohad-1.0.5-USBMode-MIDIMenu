# Troubleshooting - DS5Dongle by Ohad v1.0.4

## Controller does not connect
Try switching to another slot or wiping all slots from Settings, then pair the controller again.

## Audio is missing or broken
Verify the computer selected the dongle as the audio device. If the problem appears after saving settings and disconnecting, use the v1.0.4 Stable AudioFix baseline.

## Controller shuts down while listening to music
Enable AudioKeep in Settings.

## Remap changes do not apply
Make sure `Remap on/off` is ON. After changing a row, save with Triangle and wait for the `Saved` confirmation.

## OLED is off
Press `KEY0` or `KEY1`. Also check `ScrDim` and `ScrOff` in Settings.

## Settings do not persist
Save with Triangle and avoid unplugging the Pico immediately after saving. Use the safe poweroff path when disconnecting the controller.
